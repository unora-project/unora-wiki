Welcome to **Unora: Elemental Harmony** — a free-to-play online RPG set in a world shaped by gods, elements, and endless adventure. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to begin your journey as an Aisling.

---

## Download & Install

1. Download the **UnoraLaunchpad** from [GitHub](https://github.com/Jinori/UnoraLaunchpad/releases/tag/v3.3.2)
2. Run **UnoraLaunchpad.exe** — it will automatically download and install the game client
3. Create an account and log in

!!! tip

    Join the [Unora Discord](https://discord.gg/WkqbMVvDJq) for community help, updates, and to connect with other players.

---

## Creating Your Character

When you first log in, you'll create your Aisling (character). After character creation, you'll arrive in **Mileth** — the starting town and your home base for the early game.

---

## The Tutorial

Your adventure begins at the **Mileth Inn** with an NPC named **Riona**. She'll guide you through the basics of Unora:

### Riona's Rat Problem
Speak to Riona and help her deal with the rats in the inn. Kill 5 rats walking around to complete the first task.

### Getting Your First Weapon
Riona sends you to **Callo**, the weaponsmith at coordinates (5,38) in Mileth. Callo asks you to collect **6 branches** outside of town to craft you a **stick** and a **wooden shield**.

!!! tip

    Walk around outside of the armorsmith (13,24 area) and look for **reactor tiles** — spots on the ground that trigger when you walk over them. Once you find a branch tile, walk on and off it repeatedly to collect all 6 branches quickly.

### Choosing Your Class
Riona sends you to the **Temple of Choosing** at (9,5) in Mileth Village Way. Inside, speak to **Aoife** and enter the Inner Sanctum where five class avatars await. Speak to each one to learn about their path, then dedicate yourself to a class.

### Your First Combat Quest
After choosing your class, Riona directs you to **Skarn** at (58,25) near the Mileth Crypt. Accept the **Slayer of the Crypt** quest — your first real combat challenge. Clear the crypt and return to Skarn.

### Your First Mount
Next, Riona gives you a **mount** (yours to keep permanently!) and sends you to **Josephine** the stylist at (51,49). Josephine rewards you with gold, experience, game points, and a new weapon. Double-click the saddle in your inventory to ride your mount.

### Pick a Profession
Riona then asks you to learn a crafting profession. Choose from 8 different trades and learn it from the appropriate NPC. Once you've learned a profession, report back to Riona.

### Reach Level 11
Finally, Riona asks you to reach **level 11** through hunting or questing. Return to her once you've leveled up for a reward, and the tutorial is complete!

## Classes

Every Aisling starts as a **Peasant** and must choose a class at the Temple of Choosing during the tutorial. There are 5 classes to choose from:

| Class | Role |
|-------|------|
| **Warrior** | Melee fighter — your enemies fall to your blades with fury. Fighting is your forte. |
| **Monk** | Martial artist — channel elemental forces through discipline and inner strength. |
| **Priest** | Healer — a divine conduit of healing and protection, supporting allies through faith. |
| **Rogue** | Assassin — strike from the shadows with cunning traps and precision attacks. |
| **Wizard** | Spellcaster — master of the arcane arts, bending elements for devastating attacks. |

Each class has a unique set of **skills** and **spells** that define their playstyle. Visit the [Classes](/classes) section for detailed information on each class's abilities.

## Early Game Progression

After completing the tutorial, here's what to focus on:

### Questing
Unora's quests are organized into **Circles** that represent your progression:

- **Circle 1** — Your starting quests, including the tutorial. These teach you the basics and reward you with essential gear and experience.
- **Circle 2 through 5** — Progressively harder quest lines that unlock as you grow stronger.
- **Side Quests** — Optional quests for extra rewards, including slayer quests and events.

Check out the full [Quest Guide](/quests) for walkthroughs and rewards.

### Hunting Grounds
Combat is a core part of leveling up. Unora has **30 hunting areas** ranging from beginner to grandmaster difficulty. Start with areas appropriate for your level:

- **Mileth Crypt** — Your first hunting ground from the tutorial
- **Mythic Areas** — A series of 10 themed zones (Floppy Farms, Garden, Woodlands, and more) with scaling difficulty from level 9 all the way to Grandmaster

Visit the [Hunting Grounds](/hunting) page for a full list of areas, recommended levels, and what you'll find there.

### Equipment
As you level up, you'll need better gear. Equipment in Unora includes weapons, armor, accessories, gauntlets, shields, and more. Gear is class-specific, so make sure you're looking for items that match your class. Browse the full [Equipment Database](/equipment) to plan your upgrades.

## Professions & Crafting

Unora features **8 crafting professions** that let you create useful items:

| Profession | What You Craft |
|------------|---------------|
| **Alchemy** | Potions and extracts |
| **Armorsmithing** | Armor and protective gear |
| **Weaponsmithing** | Weapons |
| **Enchanting** | Magical enhancements |
| **Cooking** | Food with stat-boosting effects |
| **Fishing** | Catch fish for cooking and other uses |
| **Foraging** | Gather materials from the wild |
| **Jewelcrafting** | Rings, necklaces, and earrings |

You'll pick your first profession during the tutorial. Explore the [Professions](/professions) section for recipes and details.

## Religion & Blessings

Unora's world is watched over by four Goddesses, each offering unique **blessings** to their followers:

| Goddess | Domain |
|---------|--------|
| **Miraelis** | Light and Life |
| **Serendael** | Fortune and Harmony |
| **Skandara** | War and Conflict |
| **Theselene** | Moon and Mystery |

Devoting yourself to a goddess grants access to powerful blessings that cost **faith** to activate. Learn more in the [Religion](/religion) section.

## Towns & The World

Unora is made up of **8 major towns**, each with their own NPCs, shops, and services:

- **Mileth** — The starting town with all essential NPCs
- **Abel** — A coastal trading hub
- **Piet** — A mountain fortress
- **Loures** — The grand capital city
- **Undine** — A mystical lakeside village
- **Suomi** — A rural farming community
- **Rucesion** — A city of arcane knowledge
- **Tagor** — A desert outpost

Each town has banks, magic masters, bartenders, quest givers, and specialty shops. Explore the [Towns](/towns) section for NPC locations and shop inventories.
