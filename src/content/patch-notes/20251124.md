🦃 Thanksgiving Events – Patch Notes 🍂
🦚 Forgotten Peacock Event

- The Forgotten Peacock will spawn in a random town every 3 hours.
- All nearby players will receive rewards regardless of group.
- Only one Forgotten Peacock can exist in the world at a time.
(This replaces the old Ant Queen sprite.)

⚔️ Thanksgiving Challenge

- Visit Dealos in Grassy Fields to participate in the Thanksgiving Challenge.
- Endure 20 waves of random monsters to win exclusive rewards!

🗺️ Chloe’s Thanksgiving Treasure Hunt

- Speak with Chloe in the Undine Tavern to start the treasure hunt.
- Complete five treasure hunts to receive a special Thanksgiving reward.

🍽️ Food Donation – Community EXP Buff

- Thanksgiving is a time for giving! The Beggar in Mehadi needs to be fed.
- Donate cooked food items to help.
- Each donated food item adds +1 minute to a 15% world EXP buff.
- Buff applies server-wide for all players.

🧡 Happy Thanksgiving!

From the entire Unora Team, have a warm and joyful Thanksgiving!
Thank you for being part of our world.

🔧 Note
Events may receive tweaks or adjustments before the end of the event.
