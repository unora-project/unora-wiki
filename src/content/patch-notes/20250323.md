⚔️ General

    Crafted potions, meals and divine blessings will no longer dispel or Ao Sith.

🛠️ Crafting

    Alchemy Trinket has been implemented in the game! (Still a work in progress.)

        You can receive it by crafting another item once you've reached the correct rank.

💪 Skills

    Battlecry/Warcry and Focus have had their formulas updated.

    Battlecry/Warcry cooldown: ⏳ 30s (down from 45s).

    Inferno, Whirlwind, and Wrath:

        Now consume health instead of mana.

        Duration increased (now lasts longer than a minute).

        Affected by skill damage stats and treated as assails (i.e., affected by attack speed & DMG stat).

🔮 Spells

    Cunning requires less mana.

    All curses had their cooldowns removed.

    All group heals had their cooldowns removed temporarily.

    Dark Priest should now properly receive Aura of Torment.

    Last Stand now protects you from environmental damage.

Class Changes

    Berserk → Formula changed.

    Warrior → Battlecry & Rage merged.

    Priest:

        Beanniach should now function like other buffs.

        Ao Dall icon updated.

🕵️ Deep Crypt

    Fixed an issue where chests were not giving Mithril Dice.

🏟️ Arena

    Hidden Havoc should now move ghosts near the center.

✨ Enchanting

    Fixed a typo/metadata error with "Theselene’s Shadow/Darkened".

    Female Warrior 86 Phoenix Helmet → Created & added to Picasso.
