[Misc]

    now gives aggro to who it's on instead of to the priest (like quake and vortex)

    cdr: dex / 30 -> dex / 25
    skillDamagePct: dex / 7.5 => dex / 6
    flatSkillDamage: dex * 2 => removed

    now correctly resets the wander timer of the target when applied

    will now generate aggro against bosses, even though you cant pull them
