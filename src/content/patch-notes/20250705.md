Patch Notes:

Fixed an issue with Priest pets despawning themselves.

Fixed an issue with muting. 

Fire Starter Adjustments-
Player count does not affect spawns anymore to prevent abuse.
Spawn timer increased to 60s with a %20 variance. (Previously 50s with %30 variance)
Removed %50 spawn chance entirely, it will now always spawn.
Reduced Regeneration Amount per spawn/players.
Fire Tiles will now deal %75 of your health/mana if you are standing on a fire tile when another one should spawn.

Subbing will now automatically enable Lock Hands.
