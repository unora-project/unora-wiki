🎄✨ ~! Christmas Events Are Live in Unora !~ ✨🎄
Snow is falling, bells are ringing, and trouble is brewing across Temuair! Gather your friends, bundle up, and dive into this year’s festive adventures ❄️🔥

☃️ ~ Frosty Challenge ~ ☃️
Calling all Aislings! Test your reflexes in the Frosty Challenge, a fast-paced minigame where you must dodge charging reindeer 🦌 and explosive surprises 💣!

🎿 Ride the lift in Mount Merry to reach the challenge.
⏱️ Survive as long as you can to climb the leaderboard!
🎁 Skilled survivors will earn special rewards.

🏆 Top 3 Leaderboard Rewards
🥇 1st Place — 500 million Experience
🥈 2nd Place — 250 million Experience
🥉 3rd Place — 100 million Experience
✨ Experience rewards may be adjusted for non-mastered players.

🎅 ~ Santa Needs Help! ~ 🎅
Oh no! Santa has fallen ill once again 🤒 — and worse yet, Rudolph is missing!
Some elves whisper that a massive snow pile dragged him into the woods… but that can’t be true… right?

🦌 Seek out Rudolph and help Santa save Christmas!

🐣 ~ Mother Erbie ~ 🐣
Mother Erbie is beside herself — her babies have run off! ❄️
She fears they’re cold, lonely and scared.

💬 Speak with Mother Erbie quickly and help reunite her family.

💀 ~ Dirty Erbies ~ 💀
The Dirty Erbies return to the battleground!
With the elves busy preparing for Christmas, these vicious creatures are plotting to bring down Mount Merry itself!

⚠️ They are tough — come prepared.
🧝 Start the quest by speaking to the Elf in the Elf Room.

🔴 ~ Stolen Red Noses ~ 🔴
A shipment of shiny red noses was ordered for the reindeer this year…
🎁 But they’ve been stolen!

Help the Elf in the Reindeer Pen recover the noses from the thieves before Christmas morning!

🎁 Santa's Sleigh is dropping Presents!🎁
Find Santa's Gifts in towns and open them immediately! Goodies may be inside. 

🎶 ~ Christmas Caroling ~ 🎶
The towns are filled with cheer, and merchants expect to hear the sounds of the season 🎄
🎤 Go Christmas Caroling and sing to merchants across Temuair to earn unique rewards!

📅 This event begins Christmas Eve and lasts until the day after Christmas.
