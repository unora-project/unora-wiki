🧨 Patch Notes – July 4th Event & Arena Updates 🎆
🇺🇸 July 4th Event
🎇 Sam's Fireworks Show
Sam will be launching fireworks every hour at Loures Castle. Don’t miss the spectacle!

🔥 Loures Under Siege!
A Fire Starter has driven out King Bruce’s army from the Loures Training Grounds. It’s up to the citizens to reclaim the field!

🐄 Cow Herding Challenge
Knight Alistair needs help — his soldiers are occupied fighting the Fire Starter!
✅ Bring a group of 6 players
⏱️ Survive 10 rounds of cow herding in 20 minutes
💡 Alistair will give you a key hint to herd them faster!

🏟️ Arena Updates
🎖️ Arena Host & Master Host Appointment
These roles can now be appointed directly in-game! Manage your Arena team without server-side changes.

⚔️ Arena Gameplay Fixes
Lots of improvements and bug fixes to support the new Arena Host system.

🚫 No Consumables in PvP
Consumables can no longer be used on PvP Arena maps — sharpen your skills, not your potions!
