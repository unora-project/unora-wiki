🛠️ Patch Notes
📜 General Updates
❌ Removed hidden class-based aggro modifiers.

💥 Damage-over-time effects (Burn, Splash, Poison, etc.) now contribute to aggro and kill credit.

🧩 Hopocalypse Maze now gives EXP when you reach Level 2 and above!

🧘 Mastering Vitality now only requires 25,000 (was 30,000).

🌟 Main Story Quest at Level 99 now only requires 20,000 Vitality (was 25,000).

⚔️ Warrior Changes
🪓 Berserk and Asgall Faileas have been removed.

🧠 New Skill: Battle Frenzy – exclusive to Pure Warriors!

🎨 Warrior ability icons now correctly match their effects.

📣 Battle Cry Effect (used by War Cry) has been updated to benefit other classes too!

🔥 Inferno: Damage increased, but health scaling reduced.

💪 Equipping a 2-handed weapon now boosts all final skill/assail damage by 25%!

🥋 Monk Updates
⚡ Added an animation for Thunder & Lightning Stance procs.

🛡️ Dodge now grants Magic Resistance and improved positional damage mitigation.

🌀 Evasion grants less AC, but now also provides Magic Resistance and better mitigation.

🔧 Adjusted skill damage values for balance.

🚫 Monks can no longer wear shields – Dodge/Evasion has been rebalanced accordingly.

🧪 Potion Adjustments
❤️ Healing consumables no longer scale with healing stats.
