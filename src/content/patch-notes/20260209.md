💖 Valentine’s Day Event 💖

Event will start February 11th and end on February 18th.

🍫 Nadia’s Sweet Blessing
Nadia is blessing adventurers with a 40% EXP Boost for 5 hours!
Talk to her and bring her chocolates to receive the buff.

⚠️ This is a once-per-season buff — use it wisely!

🎈 Aidan’s Heart Balloons
Aidan is giving out extra Heart Polyp Balloons!
Help him recover the gold he spent on Nadia’s candies and earn a reward.

🏰 Trouble in Loures Castle
Flourentine’s secret ingredient has gone missing!
Help her retrieve it to earn a special character-bound accessory.

❗ Notes:
• This quest is not repeatable at this time.
• If you completed it last year, you cannot complete it again for the alternate reward.
• 🛠️ Fixed an issue where Flourentine did not take the ingredient upon quest completion.

🎁 A Special Gift from Cueti
Located at the entrance of Shinewood, Cueti has a unique gift for lovers to share — don’t miss out! 💝
