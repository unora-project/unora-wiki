Item & Equipment Changes
• ⚔️ Reworked various items to improve overall balance and gameplay.
• 🏆 Reworked set bonuses to encourage more diverse builds.
• 🛡️ Added new bracers (Leather, Iron, Mythril, Hybrasyl, Scale, and Spiked).
• ✨ Introduced the Celestial Staff, a new sub-priest weapon.
• 🔍 Endgame Basher gear is now easier to find.
• 💎 Essences are far more common while leveling and from bosses.
• 🏪 Mythic Shop offers fewer items for a more curated selection.
• 🏰 Added two refined armors to sell to Gwendolyn.
• 🗃️ Fixed items that were displaying incorrectly in the “Other” tab of the bank.
• 💍 Mor Srad Lamh now costs only 2 Royal Silver Rings instead of more.

Combat & Balance Adjustments
• 🪓 Limbo Bashers deal slightly more damage for better offense.
• ❤️ Assassin Strike reworked to deal vitality-based damage.
• 🪤 Traps now deal less percent HP damage for fairer encounters.
• 🏹 Killer Instinct no longer works in the arena to maintain balance.
• ☄️ Meteor Fire deals more damage, but no longer scales with percent HP.
• ⚡ Void Jolt and Zap have higher base damage, though reduced MP scaling.
• 🔥 Deo Searg/Meall and Strioch Bais deal less damage and have a lower cooldown.
• 🕯️ Shadow Touch deals less damage and heals for less.
• 🐾 Pets have been buffed with increased damage and survivability.
• 🔮 All mobs gain a 25% increase to their current magic resistance.

Skill & Spell Updates
• 🔁 Skills/Spells once again remove their previous ability automatically.
• 📖 Skill/Spell descriptions now follow a consistent format.
• 🛡️ Fixed upgraded Nyx Shield set bonuses so they work properly.
• 🧪 Adjusted Knowledge/Strong Knowledge potion recipes for better crafting.
• 🚫 Fas Deireas is no longer a target spell.
• 🍵 Cleansing Brew now properly removes the correct debuffs.
• 🎯 Potent Accuracy Potion displays the correct requirements in the Recipe Library.

Bug Fixes & Improvements
• 🛠️ Fixed a bug when removing the Resilient enchantment that caused errors.
• ⚙️ Fixed an issue with Ancient Enchant missing 1% spell damage.
• 🚪 Fixed a Portal Trinket level check bug that prevented proper use.
• 👻 Fixed an arena corner porting issue.
• 🔧 Corrected a message in weaponsmithing recipe books.
• 🏚️ Fixed an issue where “Flee 2nd Floor” dialog option appeared on F1 outside Eingren Manor.

New Content & Miscellaneous
• 🏯 Added dialogs to enter the Inner Arena.
• 🪙 Bounty Board rewards now include higher-tier crafting material boxes.
• 🗝️ Deep Crypt Chest offers new equipment rewards and crafting material boxes.
• 🏹 Killer Instinct was removed from arena usage for balance (reiterated above).
• 🍾 Adjusted Knowledge/Strong Knowledge potion recipes (reiterated) for clarity.
