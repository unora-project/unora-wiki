📜 Patch Notes
🧠 "Look" Skill Added
You can now learn the Look skill from Nisk! Use it to see your map and instance ID.

🌊 Paradise Underwater Fix
Fixed an issue where the tornado would remain after the boss was defeated.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Arena Group Size Fix

Riteforge Arena now has a group size cap of 13.

Other arena maps remain uncapped.

🎓 Mentoring System Activated
Mentoring players is now fully available! Guide others and leave your mark.

🌿 Map & Foraging Fixes

Fixed issues with Abel Port.

Fixed and added foraging spots.

⚗️ Alchemy Improvements

Fixed issues with flags for certain recipes.

Fixed missing animation for some alchemy crafts.

📚 Experience Buff Changes

🧪 GM Knowledge and 🛐 Religion XP Buff can now stack, but both are reduced to 15% (down from 25%).

🚫 Experience Buffs are now capped at 75% total. You can't exceed this no matter how you stack them.

👹 Boss Revival Enhancement
The Pentagram Boss and Porte Forest Boss now revive your group on kill.
