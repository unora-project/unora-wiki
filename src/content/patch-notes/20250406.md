🛡️ Arena Updates

    🌀 Teleport with Terminus: Players can now teleport to the Arena from anywhere using Terminus when a host announces a match — except from Jail.

    📣 Host Shouts Fixed: Hosts’ worldshouts for the arena have been tweaked.

    👥 Group Limit Removed: There’s no longer a max group size in the arena!

    🚪 Auto Leave on Exit: In groups larger than 6, leaving the arena will automatically remove you from the group.

    🤝 Team Safety: Friendly fire is now disabled between teammates in the arena.

    🧪 Effect Persistence: Certain effects will now persist through arena deaths.

🕵️‍♂️ Hidden Havoc

    ↔️ Spawn Separation: Contestants will now be placed at least 3 tiles apart at the start.

    🤐 No Talking Allowed: Talking = Death. Stay silent or face the consequences.

⌨️ Typing Arena

    🐾 Scaling Movement: Monster walk speed now correctly scales with wave count. Expect them to move faster over time!

🚋 Escort Mode

    ⏱️ Shorter Respawn: Base respawn time is now just 5 seconds.

    🛡️ Defenders: Only gain +4s respawn penalty per death.

    ⚔️ Attackers: Still gain +10s per death while pushing the cart.

📜 Quests

    🐺 Werewolf Safety: You will only become a Werewolf on quest-related maps.

👹 Monsters

    🧠 Smarter Casting: Monsters now cast more while at range and less while in melee range.

🧍‍♂️ Villagers

    🚫 Less Wanderlust: Villagers no longer randomly follow Aislings around.

✨ Spells

    💚 Healing Fixed:

        Healing Aura and Regeneration were incorrectly applying double HealBonus and HealBonusPct. Now fixed.

        🩹 Fixed a major bug where any heal could heal absurd amounts or even instantly kill you. Yikes! That's patched.
