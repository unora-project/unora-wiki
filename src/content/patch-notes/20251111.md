🩵 PATCH NOTES 🩵

🐛 BUG FIXES

⚡ Fixed a bug causing Lightning/Thunder Stance to apply crits to yourself instead of the target.
👻 Fixed an issue where Limbo bosses would not move, attack, or die properly.
💀 Corrected the Sinister Enchantment description.
🌋 Fixed icon/stacking issue with Quake / Inferno and Dmg Trinket / Torment.
🛒 Fixed issue with Nyx Buy Shop displaying incorrect cost values.

🗺️ HUNTING ZONES

🐉 Kelrak is now twice as rewarding — increased EXP and loot.
🧙‍♂️ Limbo Wizards now randomly pick 2 elements and cast only those instead of all 4.

🏰 GUILDS

💰 Guild tax rates now persist through server restarts.

⚔️ MONSTERS

💀 Damage Game Monster adjustments:
• AC: 25 ➜ 0
• Element: Random ➜ Undead
• HP: 4M ➜ 8M
• Can now be afflicted by all status effects

💫 REGENERATION & SYSTEM TWEAKS

❤️ Health Regeneration — Interval: 8s ➜ 6s
🔵 Mana Regeneration — Interval: 8s ➜ 6s
• Overall: Higher regen for lower levels/new masters, slightly less for high masters/GMs
💓 More tweaks to Heartbeat system — improved consistency.
⏳ Cooldowns shorter than 1 second no longer display a visible timer.

🧙‍♂️ WIZARD CHANGES

🌪️ Wizards may now only attune to 2 elements at once. Speak to Dar to change elements.
🌀 SAL Splash Effect — ticks 3× faster, but deals ⅓ damage per tick (same total DPS).
🔥 All Ard elemental spells now have consistent mana scaling and costs.
🌩️ Chain Lightning removed — replaced with Call Lightning.
☄️ Meteor, Call Lightning, Tidal Wave, and Seismic Shift are now obtained from Lady Phoenix.
💥 Arcane Explosion and Uninterrupted Channeling are obtained from the Master Dungeon.
🪄 Uninterrupted Channeling — increases damage by +5% per second, up to +25%. Ends if you move or take damage.
🔱 Ard Lamhs are now learnable by Wizards (even if you no longer have normal Ards).
🌠 All 4 GM spells now have a 30s cooldown and have been buffed.
☄️ Meteor now applies Burn.
⚖️ Adjusted various non-master elemental spells for balance and consistency across tiers.

🙏 PRIEST CHANGES

✝️ Strioch & Deo spells:
• ⚔️ Significantly reduced damage (except Deo Lamh, unchanged)
• 💧 Significantly reduced mana cost
• 📜 Both now deal equal damage

🗡️ ROGUE CHANGES

💥 Increased damage for all stab-type attacks:
Gut, Stab and Twist, Skewer, Stab Twice, Rupture

🛡️ WARRIOR CHANGES

🌪️ Whirlwind — HP scaling 0 ➜ 0.125 (half of Inferno)
⚔️ Slight overall increase to Master skill damage

🥋 MONK CHANGES

🔥 Smoke/Flame Stance — now toggleable, and more effective at lower HP.
💨 Mist Stance — HP scaling 0 ➜ 0.25 (half of Tide Stance).
💉 Siphoning — damage dealt is now Pure Damage.
