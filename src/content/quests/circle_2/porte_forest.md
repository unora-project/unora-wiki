# Porte Forest

## Requirements

- Must be between level 21-40 (once you're 41, you can no longer complete this quest as Porte Forest has a maximum level of 40)
- A party — the areas to hunt required items are dangerous solo. You will want a Priest for the final boss

??? tip "Watch your level!"

    If you're 40 and close to 41, press "F1" to open the Terminus menu and select "Toggle Experience Gain" > "No" to disable gaining experience. This will allow you to fully complete the quest.

!!! bug

    If you level to 41 while inside of Porte Forest, you will not be able to leave without the assistance of a GM or a passport. Be mindful of this if you're level 40 and especially during quest turn-ins, as the experience rewards may level you while inside.

## Rewards

- Legend mark: Saved the Daughter of Porte Forest
- (Missable) Legend mark: Eased the suffering of Porte Forest
- Cosmetic: Change the color of your mount cloak to purple

## Walkthrough

### Speaking with Torbjorn

!!! tip "Pre-hunt to save time"

    You can have quest items ahead of time. You'll need **4 Trent Roots** and **5 Silver Wolf Mane Hairs** for each party member.

1. Go to the Suomi Weapon Shop **(11,55)**
2. In front of Torbjorn, say "Porte Forest" — he will request 4 Trent Roots (everyone in your party needs them)

### Hunting Trees and Wolves

3. Go to Porte Forest 3-R (3-C has Trents too)
    - From the entrance: right, up, up
4. Kill Tree Demons to collect **4 Trent Roots** for everyone in the party

!!! note "Work ahead"

    While you're here, head to Porte Forest 4-R and collect Silver Wolf Mane Hairs now to save a trip back later.

5. Head to Porte Forest 4-R (from 3-R, go to **(27,1)**)
6. Kill Silver Wolves to collect **5 Silver Wolf Mane Hairs** for everyone
7. Return to Torbjorn in Suomi **(11,55)** and say "Porte Forest" to turn in the Trent Roots
8. Head to Bertil, the Suomi White Magic Master **(16,86)** — she will request 5 Silver Wolf Mane Hairs (everyone in your party needs them)

### Finding the Pendant

9. Head to Porte Forest from the main world map

??? tip "Map Explanation"

    Porte Forest is split up into three paths: "L" (Left), "C" (Center), and "R" (Right)

10. Find the pendant in Porte Forest 4-R **(11,25)** (from 3-R, go to **(27,1)**)
11. Return to Bertil **(16,86)** to turn in the Silver Wolf Mane Hairs — you'll receive **Silver Wolf Leather**

!!! warning

    Make sure everyone in your party has the Silver Wolf Leather in their inventory. It significantly reduces the boss's damage.

### Trap Room

!!! warning "Party required!"

1. Go to Isabelle in Porte Forest **(21,38)** at the entrance
2. Click "Turuc Pendant" and go through the prompts

> Isabelle mentions that you can use **Giant Ant Wings** to avoid the traps ahead.

3. Head back to Porte Forest 4-C (from the entrance: go straight up and follow the path)
4. Make sure your **entire group** is in 4-C before proceeding
5. Go north one map to enter the trap room
6. Walk in a line with your party, dropping Giant Ant Wings in front of the leader to pad the traps

??? tip "Rogues: Detect Traps"

    Rogues can see traps with the Detect Traps skill. If you have a Rogue, let them lead — no Giant Ant Wings needed.

7. Once at the peak of Porte Forest, rest up

### Trevor

!!! danger "Boss room ahead!"

1. Someone must use the Turuc Pendant (double-click it) to teleport the party to the boss room
2. Defeat the boss, **Trevor**
3. Speak to Rennie after the fight

> Rennie mentions Trevor was her pet mantis that kept her safe. You receive the legend mark "Eased the suffering of Porte Forest." She gives you a ring to bring to her father to calm him down.

4. You're teleported to her father, Lureca, in the Porte Forest Shop
5. Choose your ending:
    - **Give him the ring**: Select "Rennie" > "She gave me this ring to give to you" — you lose the ring but earn the legend mark "Saved the Daughter of Porte Forest"
    - **Keep the ring**: Don't speak to him — no legend mark, but you keep the Tristar Ring
