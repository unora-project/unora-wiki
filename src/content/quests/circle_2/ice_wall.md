# Ice Wall

!!! example "Repeatable every 22 hours"

## Requirements

- Must be Lv30+ to start the quest

## Rewards

- 200,000 Experience (total across all 4 parts)

## Walkthrough

### Part 1: Ice Samples

1. Head to the Wilderness (south of Mileth) and speak to Lilia **(234,69)**
2. Select "Sure do! Why do you ask?" to start the quest
3. Select "Sure. How can I help?"
4. Select "Sound easy enough. I'll be back."
5. Collect 3 ice samples from inside the ice wall:
    - Ice Sample 1: **(203,26)**
    - Ice Sample 2: **(245,53)**
    - Ice Sample 3: **(248,20)**

!!! warning

    The item will be called "Ice Sample X" — regular "Ice" is not an ice sample.

6. Return to Lilia to complete Part 1 — **25,000 experience**

### Part 2: Snow Wolves

1. Speak to Lilia again and select "Sure."
2. Hunt **10 Snow Wolves** inside the ice wall

!!! note

    There are different colored Snow Wolves in the area. Both colors give credit for the quest.

3. Return to Lilia to complete Part 2 — **25,000 experience**

### Part 3: Protective Charm

1. Speak to Lilia again and select "What should we do?"
2. Select "That will not be an easy task."
3. Select "I can do that."
4. Collect the following items:
    - **1 Pristine Ruby**
    - **1 Polished Bronze Bar**
5. Return to Lilia and select "Here you go."
6. Lilia crafts a protective charm for you — **50,000 experience**

### Part 4: The Abomination

1. Speak to Lilia — you are tasked to destroy the Abomination
2. Head to the Wilderness Frozen Cave at **(208,1)**
3. Inside the cave, walk to **(8,5)** to spawn the Abomination and slay it

> The Abomination is level 35, has ~30k HP, and is blindable. Most classes should be fine soloing it.

!!! warning

    You'll be notified that the spawn is on cooldown if you attempt it within the 22-hour cooldown period.

4. Return to Lilia and select "It is dead." to complete the quest — **100,000 experience**
