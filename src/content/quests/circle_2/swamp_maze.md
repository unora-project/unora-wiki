# Swamp Maze

## Rewards

- 25,000 Experience
- A Mushroom Hat

## Walkthrough

1. Head to the Wilderness (south of Mileth) and speak to Koda **(58,197)**
2. Select "I'll keep an eye out for him."
3. Traverse through the maze to find Joda

!!! tip "Maze map"

    Here's a map to find him. ![Swamp Maze](./assets/swamp_maze.png){ width="400"; align=left }

4. Speak to Joda at **(5,247)**
5. Select "You know those are poisonous right?"
6. Select "Your brother has been worried about you."
7. Select "uhh ok well good luck with that."
8. Select "Oh. Thank you I guess. How do we get out?"
9. Select "Then why are you still here?"
10. Select "Right... Well I'll be on my way."
11. Exit the maze using the hidden entrance behind Joda at **(1,245)**
12. Speak to Koda to complete the quest
