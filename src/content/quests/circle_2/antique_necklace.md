# Antique Necklace

## Requirements

- The final step requires a party to defeat the boss (a Priest makes it easier)
- This quest has a maximum level cap (exact level unknown)

## Rewards

- 150,000 Experience
- Legend Mark (varies based on your choice)
- 20 Game Points
- Potential necklace

## Walkthrough

### Starting the Quest

1. Head to Eingren Manor and speak to Zulera **(12,23)**
2. Select "I'll do my best" to start the quest
3. Head inside the manor — you need to find a necklace for Zulera

### Clue Locations

Search each side room for clues about the necklace's location:

| Clue | Manor Room | Room Coords | Clue Location |

### Boss Room

!!! danger "Boss room ahead!"

!!! warning "Party required!"

1. Head to Manor Room 8 **(11,1)**
2. At **(2,2)** in the corner is the orange cushion mentioned in Clue 4
3. Make sure your entire party is present
4. Walk towards the cushion — this summons 4 ghosts
5. Defeat all 4 ghosts

### Return to Zulera

1. Return to Zulera and choose your reward:
    - **"Yes, here you go."** — Legend mark: "Returned Zulera's Heirloom" (light blue)
    - **"It's nice. I'm keeping it!"** — Legend mark: "Stolen Zulera's Necklace" (dark orange) + you keep the necklace
