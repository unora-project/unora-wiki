# The Sacrifice

!!! example "Repeatable every 22 hours"

## Requirements

- Under Lv99 (not sure on exact level it stops being available)

## Rewards

- Varied experience depending on the effort you put in

## Walkthrough

1. Head to Undine and enter the tavern **(19,77)**
2. Speak with Chloe and select "The Sacrifice" to start the quest
3. Choose one of the three options below:

---

### Option A: "I'll go check it out." (Scouting)

1. Head to Astrid
2. Search the area at the tips of the star — every scouted area awards experience
3. Scout **at least 3 areas** (scouting all 5 gives maximum experience)

??? tip "Scouting"

    Astrid maps draw a star across several maps. If you follow the path hugging the outside, you'll find 45-degree angle roads that form the "tips" of the star. You'll receive a notice in your yellow chat log when you correctly trigger an area.

4. Return to Chloe to complete the quest

---

### Option B: "I will slay the Goblins and Kobolds." (Combat)

1. Head to Astrid
2. Slay captors — each monster type has its own independent kill credit:
    - Kobold
    - Goblin Warrior
    - Goblin Soldier
    - Goblin Guard
3. You need a **minimum of 10 kills** of any type to complete the quest
4. For maximum experience, get **25 kills of each type**
5. Return to Chloe to complete the quest

---

### Option C: "I will rescue them." (Rescue Children)

!!! warning

    This option can be **very** time consuming. Each map in Astrid has one child that can be rescued. There is one tile on each map that has a 75% chance to trigger the rescue — meaning you could walk every tile and still not find the child due to the 25% failure chance.

1. Select "I will."
2. Head to Astrid
3. Wander around any map to find a child — there is one child on each map
4. Return to Chloe once you've found at least one child to complete the quest
