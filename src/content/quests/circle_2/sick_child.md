# Sick Child

## Rewards

- 125,000 Experience (total)
- 20,000 Gold
- 5 Game Points
- Legend mark:
    - White Rose method: "Cured the Sick Child of Loures"
    - Black Rose method: "Killed the Sick Child of Loures" (dark orange)

## Walkthrough

### Sick Princess

1. Head to Loures and walk into the castle
2. Take the right path up the stairs and walk all the way down the corridor
3. In Loures 2 Floor Hall, take an immediate right
4. Speak to Paulin to start the quest
5. Select "Where can I find this flower?"
6. Select "Yes, I'll help."

> Paulin is looking for a White Rose to heal the sick princess.

### Obtaining the Cure

1. Head to the Wilderness through whichever means you prefer
2. Collect the roses you need:

??? tip "Rose locations"

    The "right" way to complete this quest requires **2 White Roses** and **1 Gold Rose**. Grab them all now to save trips:

    - **(14,131)** — White Rose
    - **(143,172)** — White Rose
    - **(248,76)** — White Rose
    - **(144,171)** — Gold Rose
    - **(247,74)** — Black Rose

??? warning "The bad ending"

    Returning to Paulin with a Black Rose will kill the sick child, giving you a different legend mark. No detailed walkthrough for this route.

### Delivering the Cure

1. Return to Paulin with the first White Rose
2. Select "Yes. Here you go."
3. Click "Next"
4. Select "..."
5. Select "I wonder if it worked..."
6. Select "..."
7. Select "I think I see him..."
8. Select "How is she? Did it work?"
9. Select "Of course. Anything for the princess."

> Paulin needs **another White Rose** — the princess isn't cured yet.

10. Return with the second White Rose
11. Click "Next"
12. Click "I hope it works this time..."
13. Click "..."
14. Click "What else can I do?"
15. Click "Got it. Be right back."

> The princess is better but still not cured. She now needs a **Gold Rose**.

16. Return with the Gold Rose
17. Select "Yes. Here it is." to complete the quest
