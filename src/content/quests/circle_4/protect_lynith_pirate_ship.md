# Protect Lynith Pirate Ship

## Rewards

- 750,000 Experience
- Blue Cloak unlocked for mounts
- Legend mark: Protected Lynith Pirate Ship from Sea Monsters

## Walkthrough

!!! warning "Party required!"

1. Head to Lynith Beach
2. Go to Lynith Beach South **(44,19)** to board the Pirate Ship Deck
3. Go to **(11,15)** and head inside
4. Speak to Captain Wolfgang
5. Select "What's wrong Captain?"
6. Select "What does this mean?"
7. Select "Give me more details!"
8. Select "Yes, we are ready."

> You need to fight off **10 waves of sea monsters** in less than 30 minutes. If they succeed, you lose.

9. Fend off all 10 waves
10. Head back inside and speak to Captain Wolfgang again
11. Select "What's wrong Captain?"
12. Select "It was nothing."
13. Select "\*take what the Captain has to offer\*" to complete the quest
