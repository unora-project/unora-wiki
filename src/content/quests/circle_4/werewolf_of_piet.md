# Werewolf of Piet

## Rewards

- 600,000 Experience
- Legend mark: Learned to control the Werewolf Curse
- Spell: Werewolf Form

## Walkthrough

### Eavesdropping

??? warning "Must be nighttime!"

    Before this quest will trigger the appropriate dialogue, it must be nighttime in game (7:00PM+). Cast **Nis** to see the current in-game time.

1. Head to Piet

> Several mundanes are scripted to walk around town at night. They'll enter various buildings to talk about a werewolf in town. To trigger this quest, follow any mundane into a building and eavesdrop on their conversation with the building owner.

2. Once you've eavesdropped on their conversation, the building owner will speak to you
3. Select "No but I will investigate"
4. Select "Ok, I will."

> They mention a werewolf comes out in Piet at night, but they think it may be one of them. They ask you to bring a group to slay it.

### Confronting the Werewolf

??? danger "Combat incoming!"

    If you are in a party and your party member has done this quest before, they will not teleport into the room with you. This room is instanced — those outside your party cannot enter and assist.

1. Head to the Piet Empty Room **(30,12)**
2. Watch as Toby transforms into a werewolf
3. Slay the werewolf
4. Toby transforms back — speak to him
5. Exit the empty room

> Toby thanks you for turning him back. He's been trying to cure or control his form without success. He suggests speaking to Appie, the Piet Magic Shop owner. He also mentions you now have the werewolf curse.

!!! warning

    Until you complete this quest, you will transform into a werewolf during the night.

### Finding a Cure

1. Head to Appie in the Piet Magic Shop **(44,50)**
2. Select "Werewolf Cure"
3. Select "How do I control it?"
4. Select "I will be careful."
5. Select "I'll find the Rose of Sharon."

> Appie says there's a rare flower deep in the Werewolf Woods in Shinewood Forest. The woods are guarded by werewolves, but they won't bother you if you look like one. He warns you to be cautious of the master werewolf, who is keener to your smell.

### Obtaining the Rose of Sharon

??? warning "Party required!"

    You need a party to teleport into the werewolf boss room. Make sure anyone not joining the quest is removed from the party.

1. Travel to Shinewood Forest
2. Navigate through the forest:
    - Shinewood 1 → 2 → 3 → 4 (head north each time)
    - Shinewood 4 → 5 (head east)
    - Shinewood 5 → 6 → 7 (head south each time)

??? danger "Potential hostile area ahead!"

    If you are currently a werewolf, the werewolves in this area will ignore you. If it's daylight and you aren't a werewolf, they will be aggressive.

3. Enter the Werewolf Woods at **(43,48)**
4. Head straight south to Werewolf Woods 2

!!! danger "Boss room ahead — make sure your party is close by!"

5. Walk into the area to trigger a pop-up — select "I am ready."
6. Slay the **Master Werewolf**
7. Head to **(0,10)** to enter Werewolf Woods 3
8. Pick up the **Rose of Sharon** by walking over it at **(7,6)**

### Curing the Werewolf Curse

1. Return to Appie at the Piet Magic Shop
2. Select "Werewolf Cure"
3. Select "Yes, here it is." to complete the quest

> Appie drops the flower in a bottle, swirls it around, and has you drink it. You still have the werewolf curse, but you can now **control it**.
