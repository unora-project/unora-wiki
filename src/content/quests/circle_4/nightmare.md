## Female Wizard

1. Head to the Piet Priestess **(30,55)**
2. Speak to Narve and select "Morrigu Legend" to start the quest
3. Select "Where should I go?"

> Narve explains that Morrigu was the ancient triple goddess — a Tuatha De Danaan, rumored to be the dark queen of the Faeries. At the very center of the Enchanted Garden grows a large tree and a patch of bloody flowers. Sing the Song of Morrigu, but be careful — she will claim you.

4. Head to the East Woodlands and enter the Enchanted Gardens
5. Head to **(50,53)** for a pop-up
6. Select "\*sings Song of Morrigu\*"
7. Select "Black crow, over the flower"
8. Select "follow the river flow."
9. Select "The washer at the ford sours"
10. Select "the clothes with blood's flow."
11. Select "Battle-bound warriors pass by"
12. Select "As tattered leaves fall."
13. Select "You wash the garb with lye"
14. Select "of those about to fall."
15. Select "\*collapses\*"
16. Select "\*dreams of Morrigu\*"

> You are teleported into a personal nightmare. Defeat **25 Nightmares** to end it.

## Male Wizard

1. Head to the Temple of Choosing in Mileth Village Way **(9,4)**
2. Speak to Gregory and select "What do you know about these Nightmares?" to start the quest
3. Select "Where should I go?"
4. Select "Who is buried there?"
5. Select "What should I do?"

> Gregory explains that a Cthonic Disciple was buried in Mileth Crypt long ago — "The forbidden one," "He Who Need Not Be Named." He tells you to carry one each of the essences of Theselene, Miraelis, Serendael, and Skandara while you approach the coffin in the deepest floor of the crypt.

6. Head to the Mileth Crypt
7. Make your way to the center of Floor 15
8. Head to **(24,21)** for a pop-up
9. Select "Listen Carefully"
10. Select "Enter my Nightmares"

> You are teleported into a personal nightmare. Defeat **25 Nightmares** to end it.

## Finishing the Quest

!!! warning

    At this point you will have the legend mark, but have **not fully completed** the quest!

1. Once you exit the nightmare, you arrive at the Mileth Inn
2. Head back to the NPC you spoke to at the beginning to complete the quest
