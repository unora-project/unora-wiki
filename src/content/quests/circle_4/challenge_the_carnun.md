# Challenge the Carnun

## Requirements

- Must be Lv80+

## Rewards

- 750,000 Experience
- Red Cloak while mounted
- Legend mark: Challenged the Carnun and declared Victory

## Walkthrough

### Starting the Quest

1. Head to the Undine Armorsmith **(83,76)**
2. Speak to Alexios
3. Select "What's in the Undine Fields?"
4. Select "My friends and I will go challenge him."

> Alexios states that the Undine Fields are overrun by orcs that kill anyone entering their territory. The orcs are commanded by their leader, the **Great Carnun**, who challenges anyone who enters. He advises bringing a group.

### The Gauntlet

1. Travel to the Undine Fields from the world map
2. Walk north **(1,9)**

> You are warned that every room has orcs. You must slay 10 orcs to progress to the next field. At the end is the Great Carnun — step into his ring and defeat him.

3. Select "We are ready."

!!! danger "Combat incoming!"

4. Slay a minimum of **10 orcs per map** to proceed
5. Continue through each map until you reach the Carnun Champion room

!!! danger "Boss room ahead!"

6. Slay the **Carnun Champion**

### Completing the Quest

1. Return to Alexios at the Undine Armor Shop
2. Select "What's in the Undine Fields?"
3. Select "My friends and I struck him down." to complete the quest
