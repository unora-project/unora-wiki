# Help Daltoo Escape

## Requirements

- If doing this as a fresh master, bring another person. A Grand Master can solo this quest

## Rewards

- 32,575,000 Experience
- Shinguards (boots)

## Walkthrough

### Getting to the Pirate Ship

1. Go to Lynith Beach from the world map
2. Head to Lynith Beach South and board the ship at **(44,19)**
3. Go down the stairs to **(19,17)** and enter Pirate Hall 1
4. Go around to get into the Main Hall

### Getting Thrown in the Brig

5. Speak to any pirate in the side rooms **without** a pirate item equipped (overarmor or helmet)
    - They will throw you into the brig — this is what you want

### Collecting Pirate Items

Hunt the pirates in the brig and collect **one of each** of the following items. If you're bringing a party, each person needs a set:

| Item | Notes |
|------|-------|
| Red Pirate Bandana | |
| Black Pirate Bandana | |
| M Pirate Shirt | |
| M Pirate Garb | Lowest drop rate |

### Helping the Pirates

Once you have all items, equip a pirate item (overarmor or helmet) and head back to the Main Hall.

**Savage Sable** — Room at **(32,18)**

1. Speak to Savage Sable
2. Select "Are you okay?"
3. Select "That's not right."
4. Select "I sure can Sable."
5. Speak to Sable again and select "I have your Red Pirate Bandana."
    - If you don't have it, you'll be thrown back into the brig
6. Reward: **75,000 experience**

**Salty Sam** — Room at **(16,3)**

1. Speak to Salty Sam
2. Select "Are you looking for something?"
3. Select "Would you like me to get you one?"
4. Select "Please stop jumping, I will be back with it."
5. Speak to Salty Sam again and select "I got your Black Pirate Bandana."
    - If you don't have it, you'll be thrown back into the brig
6. Reward: **250,000 experience**

**Jolly Roger** — Room at **(23,3)**

1. Speak to Jolly Roger
2. Select "Could I get you something?"
3. Select "Ah, no problem. I can find one."
4. Speak to Jolly Roger again and select "I found a Male Pirate Shirt."
    - If you don't have it, you'll be thrown back into the brig
5. Reward: **750,000 experience**

**Captain Wolfgang** — Navigation Room at **(11,15)** (exit Main Hall north at **(1,11)**, go to Pirate Ship Deck, head up stairs)

1. Speak to Captain Wolfgang
2. Select "What else do you need Captain?"
3. Select "Would you like me to get you one?"
4. Select "Yes Captain, be back soon."
5. Speak to Captain Wolfgang again and select "Captain, I got your Male Pirate Garb."
    - If you don't have it, you'll be thrown back into the brig
6. Reward: **1,500,000 experience**

### Escorting Daltoo

1. Take off your pirate item(s)
2. Head back to the Main Hall
3. Speak to any pirate in the side rooms to get thrown back into the brig
4. Speak to Daltoo at **(67,7)**
5. Select "Sorry I left you."
6. Select "Oh, yeah I found the way out."
7. Select "I'd love to."

> You must escort Daltoo slowly through the brig to the northwest exit.

!!! tip

    There will be flashing tiles on the ground. You must stay within these tiles for Daltoo to follow you. Daltoo also won't move if you're standing directly in front of him — step aside if he stops.

8. Escort Daltoo through the brig to the northwest exit
9. Daltoo exits and you're teleported to Lynith Beach South

### Final Step

1. Re-board the pirate ship and get back into the Main Hall
2. Get thrown into the brig again
3. Speak to Daltoo
4. Select "Didn't I escort you out?"

> Daltoo hands you **Shinguards** (boots) and you receive **30,000,000 experience**. Quest complete!
