# Piet Sewer Slayer

## Rewards

- 60,000 Experience

## Cooldown

22 hours

## Walkthrough

1. Head to Piet and enter the Piet Sewer **(57,59)**
2. Speak to Waldyr **(5,12)**
3. Select "Piet Sewer Slayer"
4. Select "What can I do?"
5. Waldyr assigns you to kill **10 of a random monster** within the Piet Sewers
6. Kill the targeted monsters and report back to Waldyr to complete the quest
