# Slayer of the Crypt

!!! info

    The monsters to slay scale according to your current level.

## Rewards

- Experience
- 5 Game Points
- Legend mark: Helped Skarn clear the Mileth Crypt
    - Increments each completion
    - At 10 completions, changes to "Controlled the Mileth Crypt Population"
- At 10 completions, you receive a bigger lantern

## Cooldown

4 hours. After 10 completions, this quest is no longer repeatable.

## Walkthrough

1. Speak to Skarn in Mileth **(58,25)**
2. Select "Slayer of the Crypt"
3. Skarn assigns you to kill **10 of a random monster** within the Mileth Crypt
4. Kill the targeted monsters and report back to Skarn to complete the quest
