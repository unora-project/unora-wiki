# Karlopos Slayer

!!! info

    The monsters to slay scale according to your current level.

## Rewards

- Experience scaled to your level

## Cooldown

22 hours

## Walkthrough

1. Speak to Kregg in Karlopos Island **(36,34)**
2. Select "Karlopos Slayer"
3. Kregg assigns you to kill **10 of a random monster** within Karlopos Island
4. Kill the targeted monsters and report back to Kregg to complete the quest
