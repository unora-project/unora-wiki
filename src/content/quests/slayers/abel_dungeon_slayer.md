# Abel Dungeon Slayer

## Requirements

- Must be level 71 to enter Abel Dungeon

## Rewards

- Experience that scales with your level (10,000,000 at **Lv99+**)

## Cooldown

22 hours

## Walkthrough

1. Head to Abel
2. Board the ship **(44,64)** and follow the path down the coast to Nico **(24,33)**
3. Select "Abel Dungeon Slayer"
4. Select "What do you need me to do?"
5. Nico assigns you to kill **10 of a random monster** within Abel Dungeon
6. Kill the targeted monsters and report back to Nico to complete the quest
