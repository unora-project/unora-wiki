# East Woodlands Slayer

!!! info

    The monsters to slay scale according to your current level.

## Requirements

- Must be level 41 or lower

## Rewards

- Experience scaled to your level

## Cooldown

22 hours

## Walkthrough

1. Speak to Ghislain in East Woodlands **(10,13)**
2. Select "Eastwoodlands Slayer"
3. Ghislain assigns you to kill **10 of a random monster** within the Eastern Woodlands
4. Kill the targeted monsters and report back to Ghislain to complete the quest
