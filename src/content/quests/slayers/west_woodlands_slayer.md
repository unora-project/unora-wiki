# West Woodlands Slayer

## Requirements

- Must be between level 41-70

## Rewards

- Experience that scales with your level

## Cooldown

24 hours

## Walkthrough

1. Speak to Maxwell in the West Woodlands **(6,12)**
2. Select "Westwoodlands Slayer"
3. Select "What do you need me to do?"
4. Maxwell assigns you to kill **10 of a random monster** within the West Woodlands
5. Kill the targeted monsters and report back to Maxwell to complete the quest
