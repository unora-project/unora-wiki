# Dubhaim Castle Slayer

## Rewards

- Experience scaled to your level

## Cooldown

22 hours

## Walkthrough

1. Speak to Hosk at Dubhaim Castle **(17,19)**
2. Select "Dubhaim Castle Slayer"
3. Select "What can I do?"
4. Hosk assigns you to kill **10 of a random monster** within Dubhaim Castle
5. Kill the targeted monsters and report back to Hosk to complete the quest
