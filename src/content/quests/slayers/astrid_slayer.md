# Astrid Slayer

## Rewards

- Experience that scales with your level

## Cooldown

22 hours

## Walkthrough

1. Head to Astrid and speak to Phio
2. Select "Astrid Slayer"
3. Select "I'm ready for a fight."
4. Phio assigns you to kill **10 of a random monster** within Astrid
5. Kill the targeted monsters and report back to Phio to complete the quest
