# Crude Leather

## Rewards

- 15,000 Experience
- 8% chance to obtain "Loved by Mileth Mundanes" legend mark

## Cooldown

22 hours

## Walkthrough

1. Head to the Special Skills Master area in Mileth **(6,11)**
2. Speak with Elganjo and select "Crude Leather"
3. Bring him **10 Wolf's Fur**

!!! tip

    At the level this quest is given, Wolf's Fur can be found from Wolves in East Woodlands 4.

4. Return to Elganjo to complete the quest
