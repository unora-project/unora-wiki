## Walkthrough

1. Head to the Abel Weapon Shop **(55,18)**
2. Speak to Vidar and select "Blade Material"
3. Select "That's a shame."
4. Select "I can do that."
5. Hunt Brawlfish in either Piet Dungeon or Loures Sewers and retrieve **5 Brawlfish Scales**
6. Return to Vidar with the Brawlfish Scales to complete the quest
