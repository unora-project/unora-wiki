# *Antonio's Vault Squeaks*

## Rewards

- 25,000 Experience

## Cooldown

22 hours

## Walkthrough

1. Head to Rucesion Bank **(52,12)** and speak to Antonio
2. Select "\*Antonio's Vault squeaks\*"
3. Select "Where do I get oil?"
4. Select "I'll talk to Kamel then."
5. Head to the Abel Fish Market **(27,46)** and speak to Kamel
6. Select "Extract Fish Oil"
7. He needs **5 Lion Fish** to extract oil
8. Return with 5 Lion Fish to receive the oil
9. Head back to Antonio in Rucesion Bank to complete the quest
