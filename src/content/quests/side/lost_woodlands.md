# Lost Woodlands

## Requirements

- Minimum level 50+, although 71+ is recommended

## Rewards

- Experience that scales with your level (600,000 at **Lv99+**)
- (**Lv99+**): Choice of armor or random equipment (potential chance for a mount)

## Cooldown

24 hours

## Walkthrough

1. Speak to Maxwell in the West Woodlands **(6,12)**
2. Select "Lost Woodlands"
3. Select "What can I do to help?"
4. Select "I think I can."
5. Select "I have a group ready to help."
6. Select "We are ready!"

> Maxwell teleports you to an area where you must kill 4 bosses:

| Boss | Location |
|------|----------|
| Elder Mage | **(80,55)** |
| Grunk | **(119,14)** |
| Twink | **(18,80)** |
| Fire Draco | **(21,25)** |

7. Defeat all 4 bosses — a red portal spawns to teleport you back to Maxwell
8. Speak to Maxwell and select "Lost Woodlands"
9. Choose your reward:
    - **"Oh, some new armor please."** — Rewards armor
    - **"Whatever you got!"** — Rewards random equipment (one possible item is a faster mount)
