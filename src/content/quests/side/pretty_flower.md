# Pretty Flower

## Rewards

- 20,000 Experience
- 8% chance to obtain "Loved by Rucesion Mundanes" legend mark

## Cooldown

22 hours

## Walkthrough

1. Head to the Rucesion Inn **(51,42)** and speak to Maria
2. Select "Pretty Flower"
3. Select "What would do that for you?"
4. Select "I will find one for you."
5. Get **1 Kobold Tail** and return to Maria to complete the quest
