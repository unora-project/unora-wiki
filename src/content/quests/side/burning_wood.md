# Burning Wood

## Rewards

- Unknown

## Cooldown

Unknown

## Walkthrough

1. Head to the Piet Inn **(39,14)** and speak to Saskia
2. Select "Burning Wood"
3. Select "I don't want you to freeze."
4. Select "I will get the Trent Wood quickly."
5. Hunt Trents in either Porte Forest or the Wilderness outside Mileth — you need **20 Trent Woods**
6. Return to Saskia with the Trent Wood to complete the quest
