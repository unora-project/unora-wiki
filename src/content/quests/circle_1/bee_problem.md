# Bee Problem

## Rewards

- 1,500 Experience
- Wind Belt

## Walkthrough

1. From Mileth, head east and zone into the Wilderness
2. Speak to Talula at **(49,158)** to start the quest
3. Select "Having a bee problem?" and "I can take care of them if you like."
4. Kill 5 bees around the area where Talula is
5. Return to Talula to complete the quest
