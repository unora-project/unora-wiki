# Terror of the Crypt

!!! example "Repeatable every 22 hours"

## Requirements

- Must be in a party with a minimum of 1 extra person

## Rewards

- Experience that scales with your level (600,000 at **Lv99+**)
- Gold
- (**Lv99+**): Armor Dye or Random Overcoat
- Legend mark: Vanquished Terror of the Crypt (increments each completion)
- 10 Game Points

## Walkthrough

1. In Mileth, head to the Tavern **(42,5)**
2. Speak to the old man, Teague
3. Select "Here, 1000 coins, enough to live for a month!"
4. Select "What do you mean by terror of sleep?"
5. Select "I have my companions with me."
6. Head to Mileth Crypt, Floor 2
7. Go to **(1,23)** and select "We are ready"
8. Your party will be teleported to the terror room — an animation plays and the terror spawns
9. Slay the terror
10. You will be teleported to a room with a chest. Choose your reward:
    - Random Armor Dye
    - Random Overcoat
11. You are teleported back to the tavern — quest complete
