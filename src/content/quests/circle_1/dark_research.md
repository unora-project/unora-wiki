# Dark Research

!!! example "Repeatable every 22 hours"

## Rewards

- Experience that scales with your level (640,000 at **Lv99+**)
- 5 Game Points (10 at 99+)

## Walkthrough

1. In Mileth, head to Dar, the Dark Special Spells Master **(10,53)**
2. Select "Dark Things"
3. Dar will request you to bring a random item found within the Mileth Crypt
4. Speak to Dar with the item in your inventory to complete the quest
