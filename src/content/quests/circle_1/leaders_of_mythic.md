# Leaders of Mythic

## Rewards

- Experience (if you save this quest for 99, which is **highly recommended**, you will get a total of 475M)
- Permanent 5% experience bonus to monster kills
- Legend mark: Ended the Feud of Mythic

## Walkthrough

### Starting the Quest

1. Head to Mythic from the world map
2. Speak to Fabrizio **(11,23)**
3. Select "Mythic?"
4. Select "What do you need me to do?"
5. Select "Harsh. I will make alliances."
6. Select "I will be ready."

> Fabrizio explains there are 10 leaders in Mythic, each sitting outside their respective enemies' zones. Every leader has one enemy. They're hardheaded and have been fighting longer than either of you have been alive. You need to make up to 5 alliances — choosing who comes out victorious and who is forced to retreat.

### Completing Leader Quests

Each leader has **3 quests** — all "kill X enemies" or "return X items" type quests. For maximum experience, complete all 3 quests for **all 10 leaders** before choosing your alliances. There is no "meta" or "best" leader to choose, so pick your favorites.

### Alliance Pairings

| Side A | vs | Side B |
|--------|:--:|--------|
| Bunnies | ↔ | Horses |
| Bees | ↔ | Mantises |
| Wolves | ↔ | Frogs |
| Gargoyles | ↔ | Zombies |
| Grimlocks | ↔ | Kobolds |

### Forming Alliances

Once you've completed a leader's 3 quests, they will ask to form an alliance. Speak to them again and they will request you to **kill their area's named boss 3 times**. Do this for 5 leaders — but remember, once you ally with a leader and complete their quest, you can no longer ally with their enemy.

??? tip "\"Optimal\" leaders to ally with"

    The leaders considered easiest are:

    - Bee Queen
    - Horse Leader
    - Wolf Pack Leader
    - Lord Gargoyle
    - King Mantis

### Finishing Up

1. Once you have 5 alliances formed, speak to Fabrizio
2. Select "Mythic?"

> Fabrizio congratulates you — whichever alliances you made will be victorious, while the others will perish. It was the only way.
