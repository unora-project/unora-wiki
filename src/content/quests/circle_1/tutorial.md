# Tutorial

## Rewards

- 10 Game Points
- 2,000 Gold
- Experience

## Walkthrough

### Riona's Rat Problem

1. Speak to Riona at the Mileth Inn
2. Select "Rat problem?"
3. Select "I can help you with the rats."
4. Kill the 5 rats walking around the inn
5. Return to Riona and select "Rat problem?"
6. Select "Yeah, they're gone."
7. Click "Next"
8. Select "Okay, I will."

> Riona asks you to get a stick from Callo, the weaponsmith in Mileth. Once you've found a stick, come back and see Riona.

### Getting a Stick from Callo

1. Head to Callo, the weaponsmith **(5,38)**
2. Speak to Callo and select "Spare a Stick"
3. Select "I'll gather them now."

> Callo asks you to collect 6 branches outside to craft you a stick and a wooden shield.

!!! tip "Finding branches"

    Walk around outside of the armorsmith (13,24 area). You will eventually see a yellow message in your chat that says "Oh, you've found a sturdy branch! That makes X branches!"

    The spot where you picked up the branch is called a **reactor tile** — it reacts to you walking on it. If you walk off and back on the same tile repeatedly, you'll collect all 6 branches quickly.

4. Once you have 6 branches, return to Callo
5. Select "Spare a Stick"
6. Click "Next"
7. Select "I have those branches. Here!"

> Callo crafts you a stick and a wooden shield.

### Choosing Your Class

1. Head back to Riona at the Mileth Inn **(19,13)**
2. Speak to Riona and select "I got a stick"
3. Select "I am ready!"

> Riona sends you to the Temple of Choosing to pick your class.

4. Walk outside of the inn and head northwest
5. In Mileth Village Way, enter the Temple of Choosing **(9,5)**
6. Speak to Aoife and select "Enter Inner Sanctum"

> Five class avatars stand ready to teach you. Read up on the available [classes](/classes) and speak to each avatar to understand what they do. When ready, speak to your chosen avatar and click "Dedicate X Class".

### Slayer of the Crypt

1. With your class chosen, head back to Riona and speak to her
2. Select "I picked my class."
3. Select "I will speak to him."

> Riona asks you to speak to Skarn in front of the Mileth Crypt. He's been keeping the crypt's population under control and could use your help.

4. Speak to Skarn **(58,25)**
5. Select "Slayer of the Crypt"
6. Select "Sure"
7. Complete the [Slayer of the Crypt](/quests/slayers/slayer_of_the_crypt) quest and return to Skarn
8. Speak to Skarn and select "Slayer of the Crypt"
9. Select "I cleared them all."

### The Stylist

1. Return to Riona and speak to her
2. Select "Skarn"
3. Select "No problem! Be right back."

> Riona asks you to visit Josephine, the stylist, to pick up her hair dye. She gives you a mount to get there quickly — this mount is now yours permanently. Double-click the saddle in your inventory to use it.

4. Head to Josephine **(51,49)** and speak to her
5. Click "Next"

> Josephine rewards you with gold, experience, game points, and a new weapon.

### Crafting Profession

1. Return to Riona and speak to her
2. Select "Josephine has your hairdye."
3. Select "Sure, I will find a craft I like."

> Riona wants you to pick up a crafting profession. Check out the [Professions](/professions) page for your options.

4. Learn a crafting profession from any profession trainer
5. Return to Riona and select "Crafting"

> Riona congratulates you and asks you to return once you are level 11 — she will have a gift for you.

### Finishing Up

1. Level to 11 by hunting or questing
2. Return to Riona to complete the tutorial
