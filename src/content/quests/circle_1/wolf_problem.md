# Wolf Problem

## Rewards

- 1,000 Experience
- 5,000 Gold
- Legend mark: Saved a Cow from the Big Bad Wolf

## Walkthrough

1. From Mileth, head east and zone into the Wilderness
2. Speak to Francis at **(23,85)** to start the quest
3. Head to **(40,107)** to be teleported into the Cow pen
4. Find the wolf and slay it
5. Head back up to **(41,109)** to exit the pen
6. Walk back to Francis and speak to him to complete the quest
