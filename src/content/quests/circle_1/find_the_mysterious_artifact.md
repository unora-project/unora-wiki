# Find the Mysterious Artifact

!!! info

    This questline is split into multiple quests in the in-game quest log. As this quest is considered the main story quest, the entire questline will be listed here and split up into sections based on the quest log entries.

## Requirements

- This quest is available at various levels

## Rewards

- Experience
- (Part 4): Unlocks the ability to Ascend
- (Part 4): Unlocks level 97 armor and sub class
- (Part 5): Unlocks the ability to be a master
- (Part 6): Unlocks Master Dungeon skills/spells for your class
- (Part 7): Grand Master gear, Grand Master spells/skills

---

## Part 1: Find the Mysterious Artifact

To start this quest, you must come across an item called a **Mysterious Artifact**. This item is found randomly, but can be found walking around in Mileth Crypt (floors 3 and 4) or East Woodlands Path 2+. There are other zones as well but those are the most common.

Once you have the Mysterious Artifact, you must be **level 41+** and must join a [religion](/religion). Once you've joined one:

1. Speak to the goddess that you're worshipping
2. Select "Mysterious Artifact"
3. Select "Take me there" — you are teleported to the God's Realm
4. Speak with Goddess Miraelis
5. Select "I spoke to Zephyr"
6. Select "Ignatar was helped by a summoner"
7. Select "The summoner disappeared once the war started"
8. Select "Zephyr said he still roams Unora"
9. Select "Is there something I can do?"
10. Select "How do I get around the barrier?"
11. Select "Where do I find the Elemental Artifacts?"
12. Select "What about the other artifacts?"
13. Select "Thank you Goddess"

> Miraelis explains that the summoner that helped Ignatar is pinned up on the 3rd floor of Eingren Manor and has protected the floor with a barrier. To get around it, you must assemble the True Elemental Artifact by collecting four artifacts and bringing them to the Goddesses. Miraelis will assemble them with the other Goddesses' help.
>
> The first artifact, the Earth Artifact, is held by the Earth Guardian in West Woodlands. She asks you to find this artifact first. You give Miraelis the Mysterious Artifact so she can study it.

You can choose to stay and chat with the other Goddesses, although they have nothing useful to say at the moment.

## Part 3: The Goddess' Trials

!!! note

    This portion of the quest **must** be done solo.

### Trial of Combat

??? info "Level considerations"

    Although this quest can be done as early as 71, it will be very tough for non-99's to complete. It's completely OK to wait until 99 before attempting these trials, but you're more than welcome to try!

1. Select "What are the other trials?"
2. Select "I can handle it."

!!! danger "Combat incoming!"

3. When you are ready, select "I am ready."

> Before sending you to fight the summoner, the gods have designed four trials: combat, luck, intelligence, and sacrifice. If you fail a trial, you can retry every real life hour.

You have **10 minutes** to defeat five waves of monsters:

1. Hobgoblins
2. Crypt Kardis
3. Orc Warriors
4. Gargoyles
5. Slugs, Glupes, Bone Collectors

Monsters spawn all around the arena. All monsters in a wave must be defeated before the next wave begins.

4. Once victorious, speak to Miraelis
5. Select "I will go speak to Serendael."

> Miraelis is impressed with your combat prowess and directs you to the trial of luck.

### Trial of Luck

1. Speak to Serendael
2. Select "Is there any tips to pass?"
3. Select "I will try."

The trial of luck consists of **3 rounds** of picking the correct door. Wrong doors teleport you back to the beginning. You have **1 minute** to complete it.

??? tip "Trial explanation"

    You will spawn into a hallway and must run forward until you see doors spawn. Choose any door. If correct, you'll see "The door opens and lets you through." If incorrect, "The door locks and sends you flying."

    - Round 1: 3 doors
    - Round 2: 5 doors
    - Round 3: 8 doors

    If you get a door wrong, it won't be correct on the retry either. Every round is purely random. Mount up and keep clicking doors until you get through.

4. Upon completion, speak to Serendael
5. Select "I will go speak to Theselene."

> Serendael compliments your luck and tells you to speak with Goddess Theselene for the trial of intelligence.

### Trial of Intelligence

1. Speak to Theselene
2. Select "Do you have any advice?"
3. Select "I understand."
4. Select "I am ready."

The trial is an **invisible maze**. You get 5 tries before being reset to the beginning (your progress is preserved). You have **8 minutes** to complete it.

??? tip "Trial explanation"

    Drop denominations of 1 gold on the ground to remember where you've been. Not every path leads to the end — if you hit a dead end, try a different starting direction.

5. With the maze solved, speak to Theselene
6. Select "I will go speak to Skandara."

> Theselene says shadows quake in fear of your intelligence. She advises you to speak with Skandara for the final trial.

### Trial of Sacrifice

1. Speak to Skandara
2. Select "What are your words of wisdom?"
3. Select "I know."
4. Select "I am ready."

You must protect **Helpless Zoe** for 5 waves of monsters. You have **20 minutes** to complete the trial.

??? tip "Trial explanation"

    Zoe stands in the middle of an arena. Waves of monsters will spawn and directly target her. You need to fend them off to keep her alive — she has a large health pool and can tank some hits.

    On the last wave, a dragon will spawn and speak. After about 10 seconds, the dragon breathes a large frontal attack. You must position yourself directly in front of the dragon with Zoe behind you to shield her from the blast. This completes the trial.

5. With Zoe safe, speak to Skandara
6. Select "That blast hurt..."
7. Select "I will do that."

> Skandara is impressed — even though the hydra knocked you out, that's what a soldier is all about. She congratulates you and tells you to speak with Goddess Miraelis.

### Back to Miraelis

1. Speak to Miraelis
2. Select "I will be on my way there."
3. Select "I'll bring a party, thank you."

> Miraelis shares that they have verified the Summoner's location on the third floor of Eingren Manor. You will need the True Elemental Artifact to break the seal on the stairs. The summoner isn't alone — there are multiple bosses on the third floor. You will need a party. Miraelis hands you the **True Elemental Artifact**.

## Part 6: Defeat the Dark Army

!!! warning "Party required!"

1. Return to Miraelis and speak to her

> Miraelis mentions that Summoner Kades left a dark army behind and you must defeat them all.

This section is commonly referred to as **Master Dungeon**. This quest is somewhat unclear in-game, but this guide covers all aspects.

### Getting There

- **First time**: You must run all the way down to CR-21. You cannot take the shortcut portal until you complete it once. You _can_ open a portal to CR11 by speaking to Hazel at **(1,4)** for 250,000 gold
- **Repeat runs**: Speak to Hazel at **(1,4)** and select "Open Portal CR21" — for 400,000 gold, she will open a portal for your entire party

2. Head to House Macabre
3. Once at CR-21, walk through the crack in the wall to enter the Cthonic Demise

### Master Dungeon Layout

Master Dungeon has two fixed bosses: a **hydra** and a **dinosaur**. Defeating each drops a **summoning bell**. Collect both bells before using them. **Do not double-click the bells until you are ready!**

There are specific spots to use the summoning bell, and each spot summons a class-specific boss. To obtain your Master Dungeon skills/spells, **you must defeat both summoned class bosses that match your class.**

![Master Dungeon Map](./assets/master_dungeon.png)

On this map, "BOSS 1" and "BOSS 2" hold the summoning bells. Each labeled position shows where to summon class bosses.

### Class Boss Names

| Class | Boss 1 | Boss 2 |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Warrior | John | Jane |
| Monk | Mike | Mary |
| Rogue | Roy | Ray |
| Priest | Phil | Pam |
| Wizard | William | Wanda |

### Completing Master Dungeon

1. Defeat BOSS 1 and BOSS 2 to collect summoning bells
2. Use the bells at your class boss locations
3. Defeat both class bosses — a portal spawns to take you back to CR-11

Master Dungeon has a **4 hour cooldown**. To complete the quest fully, you must defeat **all 10 class bosses** (not just your own). If unsure which you've killed, visit Miraelis in the God Realm — she will list defeated bosses by name.

4. Once all 10 are defeated, return to Miraelis
5. Select "Thank you, they are very useful."

> Miraelis compliments your new master skills and says you're a true master. She tells you to come back once you are ready to face the Creants.

*To start the next part of the quest, you will need **60,000 total raw vitality**.*

|--------|----------|
| Lady Phoenix | Shinewood Forest |
| Medusa | Oren Ruins |

There is no required order, but some are easier than others. Suggested difficulty from easiest to hardest: **Tauren > Lady Phoenix > Medusa > Shamensyth**.

Defeating each creant rewards either a piece of your **Grand Master gear** or your **Grand Master spells/skills**.

> Miraelis also notes that you should have at least **25,000 HP** to survive the Creant fights comfortably.

??? tip "Elemental belts and necklaces"

    Each creant has a different defense and attack element. You **must** wear a belt that counters each Creant's element and an elemental necklace for maximum damage.

    **Belts:**

    - Mileth armorsmith: Fire belt
    - Undine armorsmith: Sea belt
    - Rucesion armorsmith: Wind belt
    - Piet armorsmith: Earth belt

    **Necklaces:**

    - Carlos, the Rucesion armorsmith, sells Wind, Sea, Fire and Earth necklaces

### Lady Phoenix

1. Head to the back of Shinewood Forest in Shinewood Forest 18

??? tip "Lady Phoenix strategy"

    **Belt:** Wind | **Necklace:** Fire

    Lady Phoenix summons dangerous tornadoes that must be avoided. Standing in them deals massive draining damage. She is very fast and constantly switches targets. You cannot outrun her — stand still facing her if targeted to reduce damage.

    The group should stay close together and find safe spots away from tornadoes. It is not worth damaging the tornadoes; just avoid them.

    She frequently casts **Hail of Feathers** for group-wide AoE damage, which must be healed through. She also teleports away briefly before reappearing with a new target.

### Medusa

1. Head to Nobis **(28,1)** and enter the Oren Ruins
2. Go through the right door in the ruins maze and get to 3-5 (named: Oren Ruins Altar)

??? tip "Medusa strategy"

    **Belt:** Sea | **Necklace:** Earth

    Medusa has a mechanic where if anyone faces her front, they will be turned to stone. **Turn group loot off** for this fight — it drops Warmth Potions, which you'll need for anyone turned to stone.

    **Positioning:**

    - Healer and Wizard at the south-east of the altar to avoid water waves that can one-shot them
    - Tank at the north by the altar/candles
    - Melee DPS at her rear and sides

    **Key mechanics:**

    - The tank (ideally a Monk) grabs aggro with Taunt/Howl/Phoenix Grasp. Tank must assail while facing slightly away from Medusa (perpendicular, not directly at her)
    - Snake nests spawn on the outer edges — 1-2 bashers should destroy them. Wizard handles stray snakes with Dall to protect the healer
    - Medusa transitions between the altar and center of the room. Stay on the altar while she's in the middle — she summons geysers that deal heavy damage
    - Snakes drop **Warmth Potions**. If someone turns to stone, call it out. A teammate must loot the potion, walk next to them, and use it while facing them
    - At ~50% HP, she teleports to the center and tiles on the altar blink light blue. The entire party has ~12 seconds to move onto the blinking tiles. All other tiles explode and kill anyone not on a safe one

### Tauren

1. Head to Mount Giragan and go to Mount Giragan 3
2. In the top-left corner of the map is the entrance to the Giragan Caves
3. In the back of the caves is where Tauren resides

??? tip "Tauren strategy"

    **Belt:** Earth | **Necklace:** Wind

    The tank picks up the boss and DPS focus damage. Tauren constantly summons ice crystals at the furthest person from him — the healer typically kites these around the room so DPS can focus.

    As the fight progresses, blinking light blue tiles appear. Everyone must stand on a blinking tile to avoid room-wide damage that will one-shot you.

    As Tauren gets weaker, he slams his axe into the ground in a 4x4 grid in front of him. Only the tank should be taking this — everyone else steer clear.

### Shamensyth

1. Head to Nobis Storage **(44,26)**
2. Walk down into the basement at **(3,15)** to enter the Nobis Maze
3. Make your way to the end of the maze

??? tip "Shamensyth strategy"

    **Belt:** Fire | **Necklace:** Water

    Shamensyth's main mechanic requires Wizard(s) to put out flames that spawn throughout the arena using Sal spells (Mor Sal Lamh, Tidal Wave, Tidal Breeze if pure). The flames are not targetable — AoE must be applied to them. Standing in flames deals massive damage, and if Shamensyth or his illusions stand in them, he heals.

    **Setup:** Before starting, the healer and three DPS each stand at a pillar. Drop a coin by your pillar (the coins help mark where illusions will spawn). The Wizard should prioritize keeping flames off these coin locations. Once the fight starts, coins despawn — each DPS drops a new coin in the same spot. The tank stands in the center, tanking Shamensyth.

    **During the fight:**

    - DPS stays at their pillars. When an illusion spawns on a coin, the DPS at that pillar defeats it quickly
    - Small fire elementals spawn and must be killed — primarily the tank's job. DPS can help briefly but must return to their coin
    - Adds should be prioritized over the boss
    - Shamensyth teleports to the center periodically with blinking light blue safe tiles. Everyone must reach a safe tile within ~12 seconds before a room-wide one-shot AoE fires. Late in the fight, the safe tile pattern changes to a plus sign connected to the pillars
