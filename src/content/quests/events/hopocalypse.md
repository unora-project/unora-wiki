# Hopocalypse (Easter Event)

This event has two different activities.

---

## Mileth Floppy Fields

### Rewards

- Legend mark: Defeated Mr. Hopps (#)
- Mr.Hopps' Box (random drop) containing various Easter cosmetics:
    - Blue Bow, Bunny Backpack, Bunny Fairy, Bunny Feet, Bunny Head, Bunny Paws, Bunny Tail, Bunny Top Hat, Bunny Costume, Female Bunny Costume

### Walkthrough

1. Head to the special area next to Mileth Crypt called **Mileth Floppy Fields**
2. Inside is a boss named **Mr.Hopps** and several smaller floppies
3. Defeat Mr.Hopps to receive a box containing cosmetic Easter outfits

> This is a raid boss and may require **5+ people** to defeat.

---

## Undine Hopocalypse

### Rewards

| Milestone | Reward |
|-----------|--------|
| Level 3 of the maze | Chick Backpack accessory |
| Level 5 of the maze | Cadburry (Backpack) accessory |
| Any level | Legend mark: Reached Level # of Hopocalypse |

### Walkthrough

1. Head to the side of the Undine Goods Shop and speak to Cadburry **(17,25)**
2. Select "What happened, Cadburry?"
3. Select "I'll get your eggs back!"

> Four bunny spirits (Hopscare, Petalpounce, Burrowglint, Whiskerflip) ran off with Cadburry's eggs. Get into the maze and find them!

### How It Works

This event works like **PACMAN**:

- Four bunnies sit in the middle of the maze; you spawn on one side
- **Goal**: Collect all eggs on the map to advance to the next stage
- **Golden eggs**: Collecting one causes bunnies to turn white and retreat to the middle (they're still dangerous while colored!)
- **Unlike PACMAN**: Touching a bunny at any time causes you to lose — even white ones
- The bunnies have tracking AI and will attempt to corner you

There's a **leaderboard** by Cadburry showing top players by stage and eggs collected. You receive a one-time reward based on your highest level completed.
