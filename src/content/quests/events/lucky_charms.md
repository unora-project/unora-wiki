# Lucky Charms (St. Patrick's Day Event)

!!! warning

    This event is not repeatable. If you have completed it in a prior year, you will be unable to complete it in any following years.

## Rewards

- Legend mark: Found Lucky's Charms
- Red Fanny Pouch

??? tip "Archived stats"

    These stats used to be given if you wore the Red Fanny Pouch, but all accessories have had their stats removed. These are here for archival purposes only.

    - Skill Damage: 100
    - Spell Damage: 100
    - Wisdom: 1
    - Constitution: 1

![Red Fanny Pouch](./assets/red_fanny_pouch.png)

## Walkthrough

### Starting the Quest

1. Speak with Lucky in Mileth Village Way **(5,9)**
2. Select "What charms are you missing?"

> Lucky is missing 8 charms:
>
> - A Horseshoe for luck
> - A Star for dreams
> - A Red Balloon for joy
> - A Blue Moon for mystery
> - A Clover for fortune
> - A Heart for love
> - A Pot of Gold for wealth
> - A Rainbow to bring 'em all together

3. Tell Lucky "I'll find them for you"

### Charm Locations

!!! warning

    Piet Sewer, Eingren Manor, and Mount Giragan can be risky for low level players.

All charms are found on the floor and must be looted:

| Charm | Location | Details |
|-------|----------|---------|
| Pot of Gold | **Eastern Woodlands** | In the lobby and monster areas |
| Rainbow | **Mount Giragan** | On the floor in rooms with penguins and deer |
| Horseshoe | **Nobis** | Randomly on the town floor outside |
| Heart Charm | **Mileth Crypt** | Randomly throughout all floors |
| Clover | **Astrid** | Randomly throughout hunting maps |
| Red Balloon | **Lynith Beach** | Both north and south |
| Star | **Piet Sewer** | Does not spawn in the entrance; you must go into the dungeon |
| Moon | **Eingren Manor** | First floor with ghosts; does not spawn in safe rooms |

### Completing the Quest

4. Once you have all 8 charms, return to Lucky to complete the quest
