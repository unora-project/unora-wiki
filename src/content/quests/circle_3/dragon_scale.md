# Dragon Scale

??? example "Repeatable every 72 hours"

    Only the final part of this quest (where you kill the dragon) is repeatable.

## Rewards

- 150,000 Experience
- Legend Mark: Harnessed the power of the Dragon Scale
- Dragon-themed item

## Walkthrough

### Gathering Clues from Weaponsmiths

Visit the weaponsmiths in each town to learn about the Dragon Scale:

1. **Mileth** — Speak to Callo **(6,38)**, select "Dragon Scale"
    - Select "What did they say?" > "I will go speak to them."
    - *Callo describes an environment with bright green grass, walking trees, and a stone*

2. **Abel** — Speak to Vidar **(55,18)**, select "Dragon Scale"
    - Select "Did they tell you anything else?" > "I will go speak to the others."
    - *Someone reeking of fish used a Lionfish to lure the dragon*

3. **Rucesion** — Speak to Marcelo **(42,34)**, select "Dragon Scale"
    - Select "What was he eating?" > "Wow, I will go ask them now."
    - *The person was eating Sweet Buns when a dragon appeared*

4. **Piet** — Speak to Gunnar **(49,29)**, select "Dragon Scale"
    - Select "That's okay." > "I will go speak to them."
    - *Gunnar knows nothing*

5. **Suomi** — Speak to Torbjorn **(11,55)**, select "Dragon Scale"
    - Select "Did he mention anything?" > "I will go speak to them."
    - *You should run when you first encounter the dragon — it's fast*

6. **Undine** — Speak to Avel **(76,83)**, select "Dragon Scale"
    - Select "Anything else?" > "I'll go speak with them."
    - *The dragon must be lured out with food — leaving it will attract the dragon*

### What We Know

The dragon exists in an area with **bright green grass, walking trees, and a stone**. The last person to lure it out was eating **Sweet Buns** and used a **Lionfish** as a lure. When encountered, you should **run from it**.

### Preparation

Collect these items to attract the dragon:

- **Sweet Buns** (cooked dish)
    - Recipe can be obtained from completing [Supply Loures Army](/quests/circle_3/supply_loures_army), random monster drops, or asking another player to craft it
    - Ingredients: 15 Fruit, 5 Vegetables, 1 Flour
    - Acorns on the floor count as fruit; vegetables from most restaurants; Flour from low-level Mythic bosses
- **Lionfish** (caught via Fishing)

### The Dragon

!!! danger "Boss ahead!"

    The dragon has huge AoE spells reaching several tiles out. Ranged classes can dodge, but melee may struggle without a party. A level 72 Wizard can solo this.

    **Make sure nobody is in the square when you start!** Once the dragon spawns, it charges into the square and casts a large AoE that kills anyone it hits (even level 99s).

1. Head to the Wilderness **(19,17)**
2. While inside the square, eat the Sweet Buns
3. Drop the Lionfish in the direct middle of the square **(18,16)**
4. Run out of the square (run until the square is almost off screen)
5. The dragon runs to the Lionfish and consumes it
6. Slay the dragon — a **Dragon Scale** is placed into your inventory

> Once you've completed this quest, repeat runs start here after the cooldown.

### Forging Your Dragon Scale Item

Each weaponsmith (except Piet's Gunnar) offers a different item for **75,000 gold**:

| Town | Weaponsmith | Item |
|------|------------|------|
| Mileth | Callo | Dragon Scale Sword |
| Abel | Vidar | Dragon Scale Ring |
| Rucesion | Marcelo | Dragon Scale Claws |
| Suomi | Torbjorn | Dragon Scale Dagger |
| Undine | Avel | Dragon Scale Gauntlet |

1. Visit the appropriate weaponsmith
2. Select "You have a deal!"
3. Select "Yes! Forge my (item)!"

!!! warning

    You must wait **1 real life hour** for the weaponsmith to craft your dragon item. Return after 1 hour to pick it up.
