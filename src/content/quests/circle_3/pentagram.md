# Pentagram

## Rewards

- Pentagram armor (level 60)
- Legend mark: Completed the Pentagram Quest
- 500,000 experience (received throughout the quest)

!!! info

    To start this quest, you will need a party consisting of **one of each class**: Warrior, Rogue, Wizard, Priest, Monk.

## Walkthrough

### Signing the Pact

1. Head to the Loures Library
2. With your party present, click the bookcase at **(1,13)** to start the quest
3. Select "Open the book"
4. Click "Next"

> Each class receives unique dialogue and responsibilities for the ritual. You must head to the summoning room with the Macabre Pentagram. Each member must stand in a specific spot on the outside of the Pentagram. To start, each party member signs a pact by entering their character name.

??? note "Special note for Wizards"

    Wizards have an additional responsibility. You must collect **2 Zombie Flesh** and place them two spaces north of where you stand during the ritual. Zombie Flesh can be found on your way to House Macabre. Anyone in the party can help collect them, but the Wizard needs them at the destination.

### Starting the Ritual

1. Head to House Macabre
2. Walk north to the Central Graveyard, then east to the East Graveyard
    - Kill zombies along the way until you have **2 Zombie Flesh** for the Wizard
3. Head to **(93,12)** and enter the house (Macabre Study 4-2)
4. Walk on the broken floor boards at **(1,13)** to enter the Macabre Pentagram room
5. Each class must stand in their designated position:

| Class | Position |
|-------|----------|
| Monk | **(6,11)** |
| Warrior | **(7,7)** |
| Rogue | **(11,6)** |
| Priest | **(13,10)** |
| Wizard | **(10,13)** |

!!! tip "Copy/paste for your party"

        Monk 6,11; Warrior 7,7; Rogue 11,6; Priest 13,10; Wiz 10,13

6. Once everyone is in place, the **Wizard** drops 2 Zombie Flesh two tiles directly north of them at **(10,11)**
7. This triggers a pop-up with dialogue

### Shifting Positions

Everyone must now shift places **clockwise two times** to these new positions:

| Class | New Position |
|-------|-------------|
| Monk | **(11,6)** |
| Warrior | **(13,10)** |
| Rogue | **(10,13)** |
| Priest | **(6,11)** |
| Wizard | **(7,7)** |

!!! tip "Copy/paste for shifted positions"

        Monk 11,6; Warrior 13,10; Rogue 10,13; Priest 6,11; Wiz 7,7

1. Once everyone is in position, a pop-up triggers
2. Select "Listen closely"

> Each class receives a different clue about where to find a Pentagram piece. The clue you receive is for the class that **originally** stood in your spot before shifting.

### Who Gets Each Clue?

| Your Class | Receives clue for |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Monk | Rogue's piece |
| Warrior | Priest's piece |
| Rogue | Wizard's piece |
| Priest | Monk's piece |
| Wizard | Warrior's piece |

### Crafting the Pentagram

Each class needs a specific **Pristine gem**:

| Class | Gem |
|-------|-----|
| Warrior | Pristine Ruby |
| Monk | Pristine Beryl |
| Priest | Pristine Sapphire |
| Wizard | Pristine Heartstone |
| Rogue | Pristine Emerald |

Arrange items so each class has the correct gem and their Pentagram piece, then:

1. Go to the Rucesion Jewelry Shop **(20,10)**
2. Speak to Celestia and select "I found this."
3. Select "Can you empower it?"
4. Select "I have to do this."
5. Select "What gem should I empower this with?"

> Celestia explains what gem each class needs (organized above).

6. Speak to Celestia again and select "I have the Pristine Gem."

> Celestia adds the gem to your Pentagram piece and tells you to visit Thorin at the Tagor Forge **(28,77)**.

!!! warning

    The **Wizard should be the last person** to speak to Thorin, as they will receive the finished Pentagram.

7. Head to Tagor and speak to Thorin at the Forge **(28,77)**
8. Select "Will you help us craft the Pentagram?"
9. Select "We will face the darkness together"
10. Select "They spoke to us, we must do this."
11. Select "Here are all the pieces."

> Thorin crafts the finished Pentagram and hands it to the Wizard.

### Performing the Ritual

!!! danger "Boss ahead!"

1. Head back to House Macabre East Graveyard **(93,11)**
2. Go down at **(1,13)**
3. Stand in your **original spots** (before the clockwise shift)
4. When everyone is in position, the **Wizard uses the Pentagram** (double-click it) to summon the boss
5. Slay the **Soul Eater**
