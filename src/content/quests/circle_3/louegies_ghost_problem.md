# Louegie's Ghost Problem

## Requirements

- Must be in a party to enter the 2nd floor of the manor

## Rewards

- Legend mark: Helped the Eingren Manor's Plumber

## Walkthrough

1. Head to Eingren Manor and enter the manor doors
2. In the Manor Main Hall, speak to Louegie upstairs **(24,1)**
3. Click "Next"
4. Click "Next"
5. Click "I'll handle those Banshees."

> Louegie is the mansion's plumber and has a ghost infestation stopping him from finishing repairs. He asks you to slay 100 ghosts on the 2nd floor.

!!! warning "Party required!"

6. Enter the door next to Louegie **(22,1)**
7. Slay **100 Banshees** in waves of ~10 per room
8. Once 100 have been slain, you will be teleported out
9. Speak to Louegie to complete the quest
