# Feeding the Beggar

!!! example "Repeatable every 24 hours"

## Requirements

- Must be level 41+

## Rewards

- 25,000 Experience
- New mount: Kelberoth

## Walkthrough

1. Head to Mehadi from the main map
2. Speak to the Beggar
3. Select "Are you hungry?"
4. Select "Is there something specific I can get?"

> The Beggar requests a **Dinner Plate**.

!!! note

    If you haven't picked up cooking yet, you will need to [learn it](/professions/cooking) from Gendusa first.

5. Head to the kitchen in Mileth **(54,33)**
6. Gather the ingredients for a Dinner Plate:
    - **1 Meat**
    - **10 Fruit** (all same type)
    - **5 Vegetables** (all same type)

??? tip "Cheap crafting"

    Go to the Mileth Restaurant at **(34,12)** and buy 5 vegetables from Oona. Pick up 10 acorns for free around Mileth — these count as fruit. For meat, you can get beef slices from Wolves.

7. At the kitchen, walk around the table in the middle until you get the cooking pop-up
8. Select "Dinner Plate"

!!! warning

    Cooking can fail! If you get "rotten ingredients," you'll need to collect more and try again.

9. Return to the Beggar with the Dinner Plate
10. Select "I brought you a dinner plate." to complete the quest

> Your new mount will show up in the Terminus menu (F1) under "Mounts".
